Blooket is a quiz creation service, it is similar to `Kahoot` and `Quizizz`.

Currently, there are `9` supported modes, including `Tower Defense`, `Cafe`, `Factory`, `Gold Quest`, `Battle Royale`, `Racing`, and more.
